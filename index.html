About us

Our story

Amber Bloom was created to bring a lighter and more modern take on mead. Inspired by cozy evenings, warm atmospheres and good company, we craft drinks that feel easy to enjoy and made to share.

We believe mead can be refined, social and approachable — a drink that fits naturally into calm moments and meaningful conversations.
